# Branding and White-Label

Make LinqMe look like your own platform. Customize colors, logos, domains, and more so your clients see your brand, not ours.

## Workspace Branding

In Settings, go to the Branding tab to configure your workspace appearance:

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Logo | Upload your logo for the sidebar, storefront, and form pages. Supports light and dark variants |
| Primary Color | Your brand's main color. Used for buttons, links, progress bars, and accents across all pages |
| Secondary Color | Used for gradients and secondary accents |
| Workspace Name | Your company or agency name displayed in the sidebar and storefront |

## Custom Domains

Connect your own domain (like forms.youragency.com) so clients see your URL instead of a LinqMe subdomain. Go to Settings, then the Branding tab, and follow the DNS setup instructions.

1. Go to Settings and click the Branding tab
2. Under Custom Domain, enter your desired domain
3. Add the CNAME record shown to your DNS provider
4. Wait for DNS propagation (usually a few minutes to a few hours)
5. LinqMe will verify and activate your domain automatically

> [!NOTE]
> Custom domains are available on the Starter plan and above.

## Your Storefront

Your storefront is the public-facing page where clients can see and start your forms. It is accessible at your subdomain (yourslug.linqme.app) or your custom domain. The storefront displays your logo, brand colors, and either all your active forms or a single primary form, depending on your Landing Mode setting.

## White-Label Settings

On Starter and Agency plans, you can fully white-label the experience:

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Hide LinqMe Branding | Remove all LinqMe logos and "Powered by" text from your forms and storefront |
| Custom Footer | Add your own footer text to the storefront |
| Custom Favicon | Upload your own favicon for the browser tab |
